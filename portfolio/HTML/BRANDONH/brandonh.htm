Brandon Homelessness.ca

Programs Used: Adobe Photoshop CS4

Languages Used: CSS3, jQuery, PHP, & XHTML 1.0 Transitional

Other Features: Google Analytics, & Joomla

The Brandon Community Advisory Board for Homelessness requested a complete redesign and redevelopment of their exisiting website. They wanted something affordable, professional, easily customizable, and easy to use for their websites visitors, with the following features implemented:

  • A system that would allow for the easy addition and removal of content, as well as an easy way to edit existing content, to keep it up to date.
  • A way to track how many visitors frequent their website, plus any other useful data (Internet connection speeds, geographical location, popular browsers, etc.)
  • A professional, clean looking design that would be easy to navigate for their websites visitors, as well as a search function so those visitors could quickly find what they were looking for.
