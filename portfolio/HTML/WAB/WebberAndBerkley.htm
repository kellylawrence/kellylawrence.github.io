Webber & Berkley Mockup

Programs Used: Adobe Photoshop CS4

"Webber & Berkley" was a mockup for a fake architecture design firm. I started this design while still in college, and used it as way to experiment with different typography, while trying to create a design that would suit an uperclass, Fortune 500 style business.

This design is currently a "work in progress". I am hoping to have it working as a functional CMS theme/template at some point, and having it avaiable for purchase on various CMS template websites.
