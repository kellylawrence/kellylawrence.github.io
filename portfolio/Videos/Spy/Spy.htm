Kinetic Typography

Programs Used: Adobe After Effects CS4, Adobe Photoshop CS4, and Audacity

"Kinetic Typography" was a piece I created for my 3D Authoring class in the Web Design course at ACC.

We were tasked with creating a short (30-60 seconds) kinetic typography animation in After Effects, using either a 2D or 3D plane.

For this animation, I used the song "Spy" from Wrathchild America's 2nd album "3-D." Due to my lack of actual "kinetic" type, I decided to take the 3D approach as it would show a different aspect not commonly seen in kinetic typography videos.
